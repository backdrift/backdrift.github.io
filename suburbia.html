Suburbia is a score for a film that doesn't exist. This was the idea that got the album started. A coming of age film.
I started the album in the summer of 2023 with "A Coming Of Age Story" and soon went into a writing drought.
I couldn't write anything for over year, as I tried to complete the album. I made a first draft towards the end of
2023, but it didn't sound good. So, for most of 2024, I did not write songs for Suburbia. Towards the end of 2024, however,
I made "After It Happened" and "Around The Neighborhood", which sparked my writing again. Then within a span of three months, I finished
the album with tracks 4-9 being written from July to September. Track 4 being the last song I wrote for the album. This is the best
music I have written and idk if I'll top this.
